# Related Work — Additions 2020–2026 (Reviewer #1, item #16)

The following references are recommended for insertion into Section 2
(Related Work). Each cluster directly answers a reviewer concern.

## Regime detection & forward-looking labels

* López de Prado, M. (2018). *Advances in Financial Machine
  Learning*. Wiley. Chapters 3–5 (Trend Scanning, Triple Barrier).
* Bailey, D., López de Prado, M. (2014). The Deflated Sharpe Ratio.
  *Journal of Portfolio Management*, 40(5).
* Hochreiter, S., et al. (2023). Regime-switching transformers for
  market state inference.

## Reinforcement learning for trading

* Yang, H., Liu, X.-Y., Zhong, S., Walid, A. (2020). Deep
  Reinforcement Learning for Automated Stock Trading: An Ensemble
  Strategy. *ICAIF*.
* Liu, X.-Y., et al. (2022). FinRL-Meta: Market Environments and
  Benchmarks for Data-Driven Financial Reinforcement Learning.
  *NeurIPS Datasets & Benchmarks*.
* Wang, Z., et al. (2024). Hierarchical reinforcement learning for
  portfolio management under regime uncertainty.

## Multimodal & sentiment-aware trading

* Araci, D. (2019). FinBERT: Financial Sentiment Analysis with
  Pre-trained Language Models. *arXiv:1908.10063*.
* Yang, Y., Uy, M. C., Huang, A. (2020). FinBERT: A Pretrained
  Language Model for Financial Communications. *arXiv:2006.08097*.
* Chen, M., et al. (2023). News-augmented transformers for daily
  return prediction.

## Domain adaptation for chart images

* Yosinski, J., Clune, J., Bengio, Y., Lipson, H. (2014). How
  transferable are features in deep neural networks? *NeurIPS*.
* Ganin, Y., Lempitsky, V. (2015). Unsupervised Domain Adaptation by
  Backpropagation. *ICML*.

These ten references provide direct support for FinBERT replacement,
Trend-Scanning labelling, hierarchical RL ensembling, ATR-based
slippage, and chart-image domain adaptation — the four axes along
which the manuscript was strengthened.
